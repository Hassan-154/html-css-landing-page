ABOUT US

Back in April 2009, I had an idea for a game. But like most people, I was too busy with real life. Besides, I was sure someone had come up with the idea and money was tight. So, I put my notebook in the drawer and went on with my life. But from time to time, I would dust off my notebook and look at my mobile game idea. Then, an oppotunity of a lifetime came my way when I lost my job because of the pandemic. Like all of us, I had time to sit and think about what direction I wanted to take my life. This time when I dusted off my notebook with my mobile game idea, I took action. I found a great developer who was also looking to do something different. As the saying goes, “Iron sharpens iron.” Together, we pushed each other and refined the idea, and he made the game you are about to download. I am grateful to Mr.Toupoc Allen for turning my dream into virtual reality and believing in me when I didn’t believe in me. He’s more than my developer. He’s family.
